ZClass - Subobjects: Define suboject types.

Description

This view allows you to control what types of objects are addable within instances of your ZClass.

Controls

Select one or more of the listed meta-types to indicate that objects of these types can be added to instances of your ZClass.

Objects should appear in folder lists
Controls whether or not instances of your ZClass appear as folders in the left Zope management frame.
Change
Change subobject settings.
