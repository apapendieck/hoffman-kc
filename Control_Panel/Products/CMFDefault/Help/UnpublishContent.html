Use Case: Remove content from public site

Actor

  • Reviewer

Assumptions

  • Reviewer has logged into the CMF (see Login to the Site).
  • Content has been published which now needs to be unpublished (see Approve content for publication).

Procedure

  1. Navigate to the content item you wish to remove from the publically visible site.
  2. Select Reject from the actions box.
  3. Enter comments explaining why the object is being removed from the public site.
  4. Select the "Reject" button. The system moves the item to the "private" state, making it no longer visible on your public site.
