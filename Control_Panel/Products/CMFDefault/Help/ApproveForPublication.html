Use Case: Approve content for publication

Actor

  • Reviewer

Overview

The Reviewer's job is to enforce the site's policies with respect to the quality and appropriateness of content published by Content Creators.

Assumptions

  • Reviewer has logged into the CMF (see Login to the Site).
  • Content has been submitted for review (see Submit content for publication).
  • Reviewer has completed the Use Case: Browse for content submitted for review and publication.

Procedure

  1. Select the item from the list of content pending review.
  2. From the actions box, select the Publish link.
  3. Enter appropriate comments.
  4. Select the Publish this item button.
  5. The item has been published.
  6. You can now Browse for content submitted for review and publication to repeat this process.
