Use Case: Browse the site homepage

Actor

  • Site Visitor

Assumptions

  • Site Visitor has already navigated to a page within the CMF site[1].

Procedure

  1. Selecting the home link or the site logo from the top navigation bar.
  2. Browse the content published there (e.g. 10 most recent News Announcements, etc.)

Notes

..[1] Site Visitors may get to the site initially through several mechanisms:

  • Links on another site, such as a search engine
  • Mailed URLs
  • Typing the URL directly
