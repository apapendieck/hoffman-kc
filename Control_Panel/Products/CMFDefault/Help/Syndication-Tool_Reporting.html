SyndicationTool - Overview: CMF Syndication Reporting Facility.

Description

The SyndicationTool Reporting Facility is planned to be added to the next release of the Syndication services for the CMF. I hope to have generated enough feedback from users who might have definate ideas which would make a good foundation as a default suite of reports. The plan is not to be all inclusive, but to provide a mechanism to generate interesting SiteAdmin data regarding the use of Syndication within their sites.
