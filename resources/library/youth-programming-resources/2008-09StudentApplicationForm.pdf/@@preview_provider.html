2008-2009 Student Application Form
Dear Parents/Guardians,
Choosing a school for your child is one of the most important decisions you can make. Today
in New Orleans, you have more choices than ever. This is a common application form that
can be used to apply for acceptance at most of the city’s public schools, including tradi-
tional public schools and public charter schools.
Be sure to completely fill out the form and submit a copy to each school where you’d like to
apply. We encourage you to apply for several schools. You must submit a form for each
child.
This is an application form, not a registration form.
Submission of this application form does not guarantee your child will be accepted. Be sure
to ask the school about its admission policies. If you do not get into any of the schools where
you applied, contact the Recovery School District at (504) 373-6200. If you are a resident of
New Orleans, your child is guaranteed a place in a public school in New Orleans.
